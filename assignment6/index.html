Orange Creamsicle Cookie Recipe


Ingredients

  • 15.25 ounces of white cake mix 1 box- use your favorite brand
  • 3 ounces orange jello
  • 2 tablespoons orange zest
  • 8 tablespoons salted butter softened
  • 2 large eggs room temperature
  • 11 ounces white chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line two large baking sheets with parchment paper
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together cake mix, orange jello package, and zest until it is fully combined
  3. Add butter and eggs to the bowl, beat together for about 2 minutes.
  4. Add the white chocolate chips into the dough
  5. use a 1 1/2 tablespoon cookie scoop to scoop out the dough. Place each cookie 2 inches between each other
  6. Bake cookies for 10 to 13 minutes or until the edges are set. let cookies cool for about 10 minutes before removing to a cooling rack
  7. ENJOY!
