Welcome to Brown's Jewelry Webpage! Here you can learn about us, what services we offer, how to contact us and the best, see what we have in stock!

At Brown's Jewelry our customer's ultimate happiness is our primary concern. Brown's Jewelers, a family owned and operated company, was established in 1954. Now, operating with four generations of family members, Brown's knows family. In our stores you will find the finest selections of watches and jewelry from all of the top designers, made with a vast selection of colored stones including rubies, sapphires, emeralds, tanzanites, peridots and topaz. We also offer on-site jewelry repair, custom design and engraving. The core of our business is integrity and our credentials show it. Our creative, educated and innovative staff will always be delighted to make your dreams become your reality. From our family to yours, we thank you for your loyalty in the past and look forward to your continued patronage in the future.
