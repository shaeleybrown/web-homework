Welcome to Brown's Jewelry Store!

Welcome to Brown's Jewelry Webpage! Here you can learn about us, discover our services, contact us, and view our stock!

At Brown's Jewelry, our customers' ultimate happiness is our primary concern. Brown's Jewelers, a family-owned and operated company, was established in 1954 and has four generations of family members.

In our stores, you'll find the finest selection of watches and jewelry from all top designers, featuring a wide range of colored stones, including rubies, sapphires, emeralds, tanzanites, peridots, and topaz.

We also offer on-site jewelry repair, custom design, and engraving. Our core values are integrity, education, and creativity. Our team is dedicated to making your dreams come true. From our family to yours, we thank you for your loyalty and look forward to serving you in the future.
