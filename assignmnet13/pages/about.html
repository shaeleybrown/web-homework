About Us

For over 60 years, Brown's Jewelry has stood out consistently in fine custom design gold, platinum diamond, and gemstone jewelry. The world of Gemstone Jewelry allows creative designs with a variety of colors and luster. Each gemstone has its own story, making them favorite choices for different celebrations and occasions. Our collection of Gemstone Jewelry would add a colorful touch to any showcase.
With years of experience in handling bridal jewelry, we have developed a large collection of engagement ring styles for various shapes and sizes of center stones. Besides engagement rings and wedding bands, we also have a wide selection of earrings, bracelets, or pendants, providing lovely accessories for the bride or her family on special days or occasions.
