Brown's Jewelry Store

Brown's Jewelry Store has been family owned and operated since 1954. They offer on-site jewelry repair, work with you for custom designs, watch batteries and bands, and they also provide engraving.

When you visit the store, you will find professional help with picking out the perfect piece of jewelry. They have a variety of options of colored stones, necklaces, bracelets, and rings. They are known for their creativity, education and their willingness and delightfulness to help a customer. You can see a ring here.
